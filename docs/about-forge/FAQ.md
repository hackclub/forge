# Frequently Asked Questions

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Who can participate?</summary>

Forge is open to builders aged 13 to 18. You must be a member of the Hack Club Slack.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">When does Forge end?</summary>

There's no set date right now, but expect it to last at least until October 2026, after the IRL event.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">How much funding can I get?</summary>

Theoretically unlimited! Depending on the complexity of your project (the tier), you can earn between 5.0c to 7.5c per hour. You can then exchange these coins for funding where 1c = 1 USD.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">What kind of projects can I make?</summary>

You can make anything related to hardware where you put majority of the work in making the hardware side of the project. This includes things like keyboards, 3D printers and custom pcbs. Projects like home servers where you are mainly working on the software aspect are not allowed for Forge. If you are ever unsure of what your project may be considered as, ask in #forge on the Hack Club slack

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Do I need to know how to code?</summary>

Not necessarily - Forge is about hardware. If your project involves firmware, great. If it's purely mechanical, that's fine too. What matters is that you're designing and building something physical.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I use a non-GitHub repo?</summary>

Yes!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Why do I have to pitch?</summary>

For advanced projects, we want the community to help each other out with advanced projects! By pitching in #forgery, other Hack Clubbers can give you feedback and suggestions, and it also allows our reviewers to check your project before you start putting in the work.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">What happens if my pitch is rejected?</summary>

You can edit and resubmit your pitch! Read the feedback and try again, just by editing your original slack message. Common reasons for rejection:

<ul>
	<li>Project is too vague</li>
	<li>Cost is disproportionate to the engineering work</li>
	<li>Project is primarily software, not hardware</li>
</ul>

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">How do I get my money?</summary>

Once your project is approved, you'll receive funding through a virtual card on <a href="https://hcb.hackclub.com">HCB</a>. Please keep and upload all of your receipts, since it is a requirement from the IRS!

<blockquote>
	<p><strong>We monitor your transactions.</strong> Misuse of your funds will lead to a ban from Forge and potentially Hack Club as a whole. If you're not sure if something is an appropriate use of your funds, ask us before buying it!</p>
</blockquote>

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I work on a team?</summary>

Each person should pitch their own project. If you're working together, each person should have a distinct contribution and their own project page.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I buy parts myself and get reimbursed later?</summary>

This depends on a case-by-case basis. Please ask in #forge-help before you make any purchases as some things you buy may not get approved or your whole project may not be eligible for Forge.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I get reimbursed for 3D Printed parts?</summary>

Yep! We will partially reimburse you to the nearest 250g of filament.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I submit an existing project or one that I previously started?</summary>

If it's already finished, unfortunately not, sorry! Start a new project.
If you've just started/are in the middle of designing, you need to have journaled the entire process beforehand.

If you're almost done designing, unfortunately not.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I double dip with Stardance, Stasis or any other Hack Club program?</summary>

No! Double-dipping is not allowed. If you have coding time for your project, you can journal it, and get coins to use in the shop!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">How will I get my 3D printed parts?</summary>

Through <strong>printing legion!</strong> It's an international network of Hack Clubbers 3D printing for each other! Check out <a href="https://printlegion.hackclub.com/">their website</a>.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">How likely is it that my project will be approved?</summary>

It's hard to say without seeing your project, but generally speaking, as long as you follow the <a href="/docs/submission-guidelines">guidelines</a> on the submitting page for your project then you're more than likely to be approved!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Are hackatime-banned individuals allowed to participate?</summary>

Sorry, No!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can we use tools like Ergogen?</summary>

You're free to use it to experiment with layouts and make design decisions, but the actual placement of the footprints and schematic has to be done manually by you.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I journal research?</summary>

Yes! <strong>IMPORTANT:</strong> Please log your research journals using <a href="https://lapse.hackclub.com">lapse</a>!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Can I use auto-routing software?</summary>

No. You must do your routing manually. 

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">What is the purpose of streak system in forge?</summary>

The streak system increases your payout according to your streak, which increases for every day you journal!

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">What is the payout increase of streaks?</summary>

|streak|increase|
|------|--------|
|3+ days|1.02x|
|7+ days|1.05x|
|14+ days|1.1x|
|30+ days|1.15x|
|60+ days|1.20x|
|100+ days|1.25x|

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Is hackatime or lapse url necessary for coding time?</summary>

Hackatime or lapse is necessary for coding time more than 1 hour. If not included, time will be deflated accordingly.

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary role="heading" aria-level="2" style="font-size:1.5em;font-weight:700;margin:0.25em 0;">Do i need a lapse building my project?</summary>

Yes, you need to lapse building it. If not lapsed we might deflate time if the work to time ratio is inconsistent.

</details>
